About Zoviko Health Care

Zoviko Health Care Pvt Ltd is Sister Concerns firm of Neha Nurses Bureau was established in 2002 by Mr. Mintu Kumar. Neha Nurses Bureau is a Noida based nursing agency, which was launched with the mission of helping the patients by providing them the best nursing care.

Neha Nurses Bureau is a leading nursing agency in Delhi-NCR. We are offering Patient Care, Male &Female Nurses Attendants, New Born Baby Care, Mother Care, Ayahs, Old Ages Care, and Physiotherapist. We are known for providing quality personals, who offer unmatched services to our clients. Customer satisfaction is our sole motto and we endeavor to consistently improve our quality of services to accommodate the changing needs of our clients.We have recruited a team of extremely well trained and well equipped staff who have meticulous medical knowledge. We have also trained them to be punctual so that they can fulfill their responsibilities within the stipulated schedule.
